## Facebook Post Generator (Hungarian)

## Task

Create a short Facebook post in Hungarian about a past political event, using the provided inputs.

## Inputs

- {TITLE} → Title of the article/event
- {SUMMARY} → Summary of the event
- {CATEGORY} → Category of the event
- {DATES} → one or multiple dates (e.g., "2011 október", "2025 március")
- {LINKS} → URL to the source article
- {TODAY} → today's date

## Instructions

- START with the {TITLE} in all capital letters
- FOLLOW with the year (from {DATES}) and the {CATEGORY} as context — briefly, one line
- TAKE the most important facts directly from {SUMMARY} — use the same wording where possible, do not rephrase or reinterpret
- FRAME the event as something that happened in the past, but point to why it is still relevant today — do this factually, without dramatization
- KEEP the post short: 2–4 sentences after the title line
- END with the {LINKS}

## Tone

- Factual and straightforward
- No sarcasm, irony, or cynicism — state what happened, let the facts speak
- No theatrical or emotional language
- Write as a native Hungarian speaker would — natural, clear sentences
- Mostly use past tense as most of the events happened in tha past
- Use present tense if the event has still effect on today or future tense if the event will happen in the future

## Constraints

- Do NOT use markdown formatting (**, *, #, etc.) — it does not render on Facebook
- Do NOT copy the full summary — extract only the essential facts
- Do NOT add opinions or editorial commentary beyond what is in {SUMMARY}
- Grammar check: correct Hungarian verb conjugation, noun cases, natural phrasing
